About Us

Welcome to KDP Docs, where our goal is to provide a comprehensive book design experience for creatives, sellers, and publishers.

We understand the importance of creating a visually appealing and professional book, which is why our team of skilled designers works diligently to produce new low-content book interiors and cover templates on a weekly basis.
These templates are specifically designed for KDP and pre-formatted files, ensuring that they meet the necessary specifications and guidelines.

Our selection of templates not only look great, but also give you the flexibility to add your own personal touch.
All of our templates are editable, allowing you to make adjustments to the text, images, and layout to truly make it your own.
Whether you're a self-published author, a small business owner, or a publisher, our templates are designed to help you create a polished and professional book that stands out in the market.

In addition to our templates, our team is available to provide support and guidance throughout the design process.
We understand that creating a book can be a daunting task, which is why we're here to help you every step of the way.
Whether you have a question about using our templates or need assistance with formatting, we're here to assist you.

At KDP Docs, we pride ourselves on quality, flexibility, and user-friendliness.
Browse our selection of templates today and discover how easy it is to create a beautiful, professional book.
We are dedicated to give you the best experience and to help you achieve your goals in the book publishing world.
